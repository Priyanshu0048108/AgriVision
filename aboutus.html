About AgriVision

Welcome to AgriVision — where artificial intelligence meets sustainable agriculture.

We are a team of passionate technologists, researchers, and agricultural innovators working to revolutionize modern farming using the power of computer vision and machine learning. Our mission is to help farmers, agronomists, and agricultural organizations monitor their fields more efficiently, make smarter decisions, and reduce manual effort and chemical usage through intelligent automation.

💡 What We Do

AgriVision provides a cutting-edge, software-driven solution that:

  • 📷 Detects weeds from field images or videos with high accuracy
  • 🌱 Identifies optimal planting locations based on spacing and soil visibility
  • 📍 Offers real-time coordinates for robotic or manual intervention
  • 🗺️ Generates field maps for analysis, planning, and precision agriculture

Our system is completely hardware-independent, meaning it can work on drones, handheld devices, or agricultural robots — making it flexible and scalable for farms of all sizes.
