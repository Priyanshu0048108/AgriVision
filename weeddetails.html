Weed Details

1. Phalaris minor (Canary Grass / छोटा जंगली जौ)

Scientific Name: Phalaris minor

Affects: Wheat

Traits: Looks very similar to wheat at early stages; grassy weed

Habitat: Moist fields, especially in irrigated wheat belts (Punjab, UP)

Control Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ (very hard)

Note: Major cause of yield loss due to misidentification

2. Chenopodium album (Bathua)

Scientific Name: Chenopodium album

Affects: Mustard, wheat, lentils

Traits: Broad leaves, fast growth, competes for nutrients

Habitat: Rabi crops, cold season

Control Difficulty: ⭐⭐

Note: Edible in small quantities but a problematic weed in fields

3. Cyperus rotundus (Nut Grass / मोंथा)

Scientific Name: Cyperus rotundus

Affects: Sugarcane, rice, vegetables

Traits: Underground tubers, survives plowing; regrows quickly

Habitat: Grows year-round, especially in warm, moist soils

Control Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Note: Considered the "world’s worst weed" by FAO

4. Echinochloa crus-galli (Barnyard Grass / सांवा घास)

Scientific Name: Echinochloa crus-galli

Affects: Rice

Traits: Mimics rice, strong competition for nutrients

Habitat: Waterlogged paddy fields

Control Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

Note: Requires deep learning models for distinction

5. Amaranthus viridis (Chaulai / Pigweed)

Scientific Name: Amaranthus viridis

Affects: Maize, cotton, vegetables

Traits: Broadleaf, fast-spreading, seeds profusely

Habitat: Dry fields, kharif season

Control Difficulty: ⭐⭐

6. Parthenium hysterophorus (Congress Grass / गाजर घास)

Scientific Name: Parthenium hysterophorus

Affects: All crops (non-crop invasive)

Traits: Allergic to humans, invasive, toxic to cattle

Habitat: Roadsides, fallow lands, crop edges

Control Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐

Note: Also a major public health concern

7. Sorghum halepense (Johnson Grass)

Scientific Name: Sorghum halepense

Affects: Maize, sugarcane, pulses

Traits: Tall, rhizomatous, highly competitive

Habitat: Summer crops, dry zones

Control Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

8. Convolvulus arvensis (Field Bindweed / लटकी)

Scientific Name: Convolvulus arvensis

Affects: Mustard, chickpea, pulses

Traits: Vine-type weed, wraps around crops, difficult to uproot

Habitat: Dry fields in rabi season

Control Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐⭐

9. Setaria spp. (Foxtail Grass)

Scientific Name: Setaria glauca / Setaria viridis

Affects: Wheat, maize

Traits: Tall, yellow-green spikes, easy to miss early

Habitat: Upland fields, kharif & rabi

Control Difficulty: ⭐⭐⭐

10. Trianthema portulacastrum (Horse Purslane / सान्टा घास)

Scientific Name: Trianthema portulacastrum

Affects: Cotton, soybean, groundnut

Traits: Grows low, spreads laterally, fleshy leaves

Habitat: Sandy-loam soil, hot seasons

Control Difficulty: ⭐⭐
